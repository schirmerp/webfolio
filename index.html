    About Me

    During Covid, I decided I was going to teach myself web development and computer programming. Since then I have been on a relentless quest for technological knowledge and skills. I started Harvard University's CS50 course. It provided a great foundation for me to build on, but I thirsted for more! I started taking every online course I could find, I even had multiple phone apps for learning JavaScript so I could learn while I wasn't home! Eventually I was introduced to The Odin Project, an 8 month Full Stack Web Development course. It came highly recommended, so I gave it a shot. It took me almost an entire year to complete, but I simply can not say enough about how amazing of a resource it turned out to be. I learned everything from HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node, Git, JSON, Web APIs, and that's just that's just to name a few. Shortly after completing The Odin Project, I was presented with an even more amazing educational offer in the form of being accepted to Per Scholas! I was offered a chance to join the AWS ReStart Program. Not Only was I able to learn Cloud Computing, while earning my AWS Cloud Practitioner Certification, but we learned tons of other stuff alone the way. Things like Networking, Security, and Databases. I was even able to pick up a new language, Python! In addition to everything I have already learned, as a graduate of Per Scholas I have access to their Alumni Continued Education programs and Learning Support for 2 years. Resources I plan to fully exhaust over the next couple years.
